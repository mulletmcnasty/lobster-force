# LOBSTER FORCE - Social Content Strategy

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Confident, irreverent, authentic, working-class pride, slightly absurd but dead serious about it

**Language:** Blue-collar, direct, no corporate speak, lots of mullet/lobster puns

**Vibe:** Dive bar wisdom meets motivational poster. Part meme, part movement.

---

## Platform Strategy

### Instagram (@lobsterforce)
**Focus:** Visual mullet culture, member spotlights, merch drops

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Member mullet photos (tagged submissions)
- 30% Memes & graphics
- 20% Merch & product shots
- 10% Behind-the-scenes / founder content

**Posting Schedule:** Daily (1-2 posts)

**Sample Posts:**
1. **Member Spotlight Monday** - Feature a legendary mullet from the community
2. **Wisdom Wednesday** - Beach bar philosophy quotes over neon bar photos
3. **Friday Flows** - Best mullets of the week compilation
4. **Weekend Vibes** - Party-in-the-back energy, dive bar aesthetic

**Hashtags:** #LobsterForce #MulletNation #BusinessInTheFront #PartyInTheBack #BlueCollarPride #NoApologies #WorkHardMoltHarder

---

### Twitter/X (@LobsterForce)
**Focus:** Hot takes, community engagement, viral moments

**Content Mix:**
- 50% Tweets (original thoughts, mullet philosophy)
- 30% Retweets/engagement (mullet sightings, member content)
- 20% Memes & images

**Posting Schedule:** 3-5 tweets/day

**Sample Tweets:**
- "Your hair has two jobs: intimidate in meetings, flow in the wind at 65mph. That's the mullet difference."
- "A lobster molts to grow. You cut your hair to conform. We are not the same."
- "If your barber asks 'are you sure?' you're doing it right."
- "The mullet is a lifestyle. The lobster is a state of mind. Together, unstoppable."
- "Respect to everyone who's ever walked into a corporate office with a mullet and owned it. You're Lobster Force."

---

### TikTok (@lobsterforce)
**Focus:** Short-form mullet culture, transformations, comedy

**Content Ideas:**
1. **Mullet Transformation Videos** - Before/after with dramatic music
2. **"Day in the Life"** - Following Lobster Force members at work
3. **Mullet Challenges** - "Show us your flow" trend
4. **Comedy Sketches** - Mullet misunderstandings, reactions, bar stories
5. **Educational** - "How to style a mullet properly"
6. **Duets** - React to other mullet content, boost community

**Audio Strategy:** Use popular sounds but add Lobster Force twist. Create original audio clips from manifesto quotes.

**Hashtags:** #LobsterForce #MulletTok #Mullet #BusinessInTheFront #PartyInTheBack

## Engagement Strategy

### Respond to Everything
- Every comment, every DM, every tag
- Authentically, not robotically
- Build real relationships

### Amplify the Community
- Repost member content daily
- Give credit, tag sources
- Make members feel seen

### Collaborate
- Partner with mullet-friendly brands (barber shops, dive bars, beer brands)
- Reach out to influencers in adjacent spaces (blue-collar content, lifestyle)
- Cross-promote with similar movements

### Host Events
- Monthly virtual hangouts on Discord
- Regional meetups (start small)
- Annual "Lobsterfest" - the ultimate mullet gathering
