## Merch Strategy

### Launch Plan

**Phase 1 (Month 1):** Core essentials
- Trucker hats
- T-shirts (3 designs)
- Koozies
- Stickers

**Phase 2 (Month 2-3):** Expand line
- Hoodies
- Patches
- Bandanas
- Flags

**Phase 3 (Month 4-6):** Premium items
- Denim vest
- Mullet care kit
- Bottle opener
- Pins

### Sales Channels

1. **Direct website** - LobsterForce.com/shop (Shopify)
2. **Print-on-demand** - Printful integration for scalability
3. **Limited drops** - Create urgency with exclusive designs
4. **Pop-up shops** - At events, bars, festivals
5. **Wholesale** - Partner with dive bars, barber shops, workwear stores

### Pricing Philosophy

**Quality over profit margin.**  
Price items fairly for the working-class audience. Make money on volume and loyalty, not gouging. $30 for a t-shirt is the max â€” this isn't a luxury brand, it's a people's brand.

### Sustainability

- Use quality materials that last (no fast fashion garbage)
- Offer repairs for denim items
- Print-on-demand to avoid waste
- Partner with ethical manufacturers
