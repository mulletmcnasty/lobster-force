Your Data, Your Rights

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

We're not in the business of selling your data. Lobster Force is a community, not a data farm. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. Written in plain English, because that's how we roll.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Give Us

When you create an account or interact with Lobster Force, you might provide:

  • Account info: Name, email, username, password
  • Profile details: Bio, location, Instagram handle (optional)
  • Mullet submissions: Photos, descriptions, and stories you upload to the gallery
  • Messages: Contact form submissions, emails, or DMs
  • Payment info: For memberships or merch (processed securely via Stripeâ€”we don't store your card details)

Information We Collect Automatically

When you use the Site, we collect:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, links clicked
  • Device info: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Cookies: Small files that help us remember your preferences (more on that below)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Run the Site: Manage accounts, process payments, display your submissions
  • Communicate: Send you updates, newsletters, and respond to inquiries (you can opt out anytime)
  • Improve the experience: Analyze usage to make the Site better
  • Stay secure: Prevent fraud, spam, and abuse
  • Comply with the law: If legally required, we'll cooperate with authorities

We do NOT sell your data. We're not that kind of company. Your email won't end up on some spam list. We respect your privacy because we'd want the same respect.

3. Sharing Your Information

We only share your information in these situations:

Public Content

If you submit a mullet photo or post to the gallery, that content is public. Anyone can see it. That's the point. Don't submit anything you want to keep private.

Service Providers

We use third-party tools to run the Site (e.g., Supabase for data storage, Stripe for payments, Google Analytics for traffic). These companies only access your data to provide their services and are bound by their own privacy policies.

Legal Requirements

If the law requires it (e.g., a court order or legal investigation), we'll share your information. We'll fight unreasonable requests, but we're not going to jail for you.

Business Transfers

If Lobster Force is acquired or merges with another company, your information may transfer to the new owner. We'll notify you if that happens.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and analyze traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features of the Site might not work properly.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Required for the Site to function (e.g., login sessions)
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how people use the Site (via Google Analytics)
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and choices

We don't use advertising cookies or creepy tracking tech. We're not following you around the internet.

5. Your Rights

You have control over your data. Here's what you can do:

Access Your Data

Request a copy of the information we have about you. Contact us and we'll send it over.

Update Your Data

Change your account info anytime through your member portal. If you need help, reach out.

Delete Your Data

Want to leave? No hard feelings. You can delete your account, and we'll remove your personal information. Note: public submissions (like gallery photos) may remain visible unless you specifically request removal.

Opt Out of Emails

Unsubscribe from newsletters anytime by clicking the link at the bottom of any email. We'll still send important account-related messages (like password resets), but no marketing.

6. Data Security

We take security seriously. Your data is stored on secure servers, and we use encryption for sensitive information (like passwords and payment details).

That said, no system is 100% secure. We do our best, but we can't guarantee absolute protection. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication (when available).

7. Third-Party Links

Our Site may link to other websites (like social media platforms). We're not responsible for their privacy practices. Check their policies before sharing info.

8. Children's Privacy

Lobster Force is not intended for users under 18. We don't knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover we've collected data from someone under 18, we'll delete it.

9. International Users

Lobster Force is based in the United States. If you're accessing the Site from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to and stored in the U.S. By using the Site, you consent to that transfer.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Major changes will be announced on the Site or via email (if you're a member).

11. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Want to access, update, or delete your data? Get in touch. We're here to help.

Email: privacy@lobsterforce.com
