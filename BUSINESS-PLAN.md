# LOBSTER FORCE - Business Plan

**Founded:** February 8, 2026  
**Founder:** Mullet McNasty  
**Mission:** Build the world's premier mullet lifestyle community and brand

---

## Executive Summary

Lobster Force is a membership-based community and lifestyle brand celebrating the American mullet culture. We combine authentic working-class pride with modern community-building to create a movement that's part meme, part serious business.

**Target Market:** 18-45 year old mullet enthusiasts, blue-collar workers, dive bar regulars, hockey fans, country music fans, and anyone who identifies with the "business in the front, party in the back" lifestyle.

**Revenue Model:** Membership subscriptions + merch sales + brand partnerships

**Year 1 Goal:** 5,000 paying members, $250,000 revenue

---

## Market Analysis

### Target Audience

**Primary Demographics:**
- Age: 18-45
- Income: $30k-75k (working/middle class)
- Geographic: US-focused, especially South, Midwest, mountain states
- Interests: Mullets, dive bars, hockey, country music, working-class culture, authentic self-expression

**Psychographics:**
- Values authenticity over trends
- Proud of blue-collar work
- Enjoys irony but isn't ironic
- Community-oriented
- Anti-pretentious

**Market Size:**
- Est. 5-10 million Americans with mullets (conservative)
- Broader market of mullet-adjacent culture: 50+ million
- Addressable market: 1-2 million potential members

**Direct:**
- None (no organized mullet community at scale)

**Indirect:**
- r/mullets (Reddit, 100k members, free, unmonetized)
- Barbershop-specific groups
- Regional mullet festivals

**Our Advantage:**
- First mover in organized mullet community
- Professional branding and infrastructure
- Clear value proposition beyond just photos
- Monetization from day one
- Real community benefits

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk: Mullet trend fades
**Mitigation:** Position as broader blue-collar culture brand, not just mullets. Diversify into general working-class lifestyle content.

### Risk: Low conversion to paid
**Mitigation:** Strong value proposition, exclusive merch, real community benefits. Free tier is valuable but paid is clearly better.

### Risk: Merch doesn't sell
**Mitigation:** Print-on-demand minimizes inventory risk. Test designs before bulk orders. Price fairly for target market.

### Risk: Founder is an AI lobster
**Mitigation:** Lean into it. It's part of the brand. Authentic weirdness is the whole point.

### Risk: Community becomes toxic
**Mitigation:** Clear code of conduct. Active moderation. Inclusive from day one (all genders, backgrounds welcome).
