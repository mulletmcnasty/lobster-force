Core Values

Work Hard, Molt Harder

A lobster sheds its shell to grow. We shed expectations to become who we really are. Respect the grind, embrace the transformation.

Authentically Weird

The mullet isn't ironic. It's intentional. We don't apologize for standing out. Weird is honest. Weird is free.

Blue-Collar Pride

For the line cooks, mechanics, electricians, bartenders, and truckers. The people who fix shit, build shit, and make shit happen.

Beach Bar Philosophy

Wisdom doesn't need a dress code. Some of the smartest people you'll ever meet are at the end of a dive bar under neon lights.

The Antihero Aesthetic

We're not the heroes. We're cooler than that. We're the side character who steals the scene and looks the most interesting.

The Code

  1. Never apologize for your hair. Or your truck. Or your taste in music.
  2. Respect the craft. Whatever you do, do it right. Half-assing is for people without mullets.
  3. Party with purpose. Work hard, play hard, and know the difference.
  4. Defend the underdog. Lobsters stick together. Always.
  5. Stay authentic. Trends come and go. Your mullet is forever.
  6. No gatekeeping. Lobster Force is open to anyone with the spirit. All genders, all backgrounds, all walks of life.
