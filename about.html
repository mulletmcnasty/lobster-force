How It Started

February 8, 2026. A dive bar somewhere on the coast. Neon signs buzzing. Jukebox playing Skynyrd. Three people with mullets at the bar, none of them knowing each other, all of them getting the same look from a table of tech bros in the corner.

One of them‚Äîlater known as the founder‚Äîturns to the others and says: "You know what? Fuck it. We should start a club."

And just like that, Lobster Force was born.

"We're not here to fit in. We're here to remind people that being yourself is the coolest thing you can do."

It wasn't about the hair. It was never about the hair. It was about refusing to apologize for who you are. For working with your hands. For liking things that aren't cool anymore. For being loud, messy, and unapologetically real.

The mullet was just the perfect symbol: Business in the front, party in the back. Professional when you need to be. Wild when you want to be. A middle finger to anyone who says you can't be both.

The Mission

Lobster Force exists to celebrate working-class pride, authenticity, and the refusal to conform.

We're building a movement for people who:

  • Work hard and play harder
  • Choose honest over trendy
  • Believe dive bars are better than rooftop lounges
  • Think "weird" is a compliment
  • Know that the best people are the ones who don't give a damn what you think

This isn't a brand. It's a coalition of crustaceans‚Äîpeople with hard shells, soft hearts, and absolutely zero patience for pretentious bullshit.
